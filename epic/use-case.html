Use Case

Use Case:

As a user, Arkin would like to possibly submit sensitive information to the site.

User Story:

Arkin worked as a Junior Systems Analyst and quit his job after handling sensitive data and is considering submitting the information to the site but is hesitant to.

Interaction Flow:

  • After masking his IP address with a VPN, Arkin opens the site in a private browsing session
  • Server returns the Wikileaks Homepage
  • Arkin clicks on the corporations leak link
  • He types in his company name to see if anyone else had submitted the leak he has the urge to about the company he worked for
  • His search did not return any related information
  • He clicks on the submit button and reads the instructions on how to submit a leak
  • He sits for a few moments, exits the site, quitds his browsing session and closes his laptop
